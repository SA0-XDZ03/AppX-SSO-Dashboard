Al-Khalid tank: This is a main battle tank developed jointly by Pakistan and China. It is equipped with a 125mm smoothbore gun, a 7.62mm coaxial machine gun, and a 12.7mm anti-aircraft machine gun. It has a crew of three and is powered by a 1,200 horsepower diesel engine.
Al-Zarrar tank: This is a main battle tank developed by Pakistan. It is equipped with a 125mm smoothbore gun, a 7.62mm coaxial machine gun, and a 12.7mm anti-aircraft machine gun. It has a crew of three and is powered by a 1,200 horsepower diesel engine.
Al-Hussein tank: This is a main battle tank developed by Pakistan based on the Chinese Type 69 tank. It is equipped with a 105mm gun, a 7.62mm coaxial machine gun, and a 12.7mm anti-aircraft machine gun. It has a crew of three and is powered by a 500 horsepower diesel engine.
Type 59 tank: This is a main battle tank developed by China and used by Pakistan. It is equipped with a 100mm gun, a 7.62mm coaxial machine gun, and a 12.7mm anti-aircraft machine gun. It has a crew of four and is powered by a 520 horsepower diesel engine.
Type 69 tank: This is a main battle tank developed by China and used by Pakistan. It is equipped with a 100mm gun, a 7.62mm coaxial machine gun, and a 12.7mm anti-aircraft machine gun. It has a crew of four and is powered by a 520 horsepower diesel engine.
